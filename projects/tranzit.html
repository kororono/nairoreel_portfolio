Tranzit

A short, fast-paced CGI film about the delivery of a mysterious case — and the clock counting down to get it there.

Year

Tranzit is a short, fast-paced CGI film about the delivery of a mysterious case — and the clock counting down to get it there. Every frame is fully computer-generated: the vehicle, the forest road, the environment fog and the light cutting through it, modelled and rendered to hold up at cinema scale.

These stills come from a single scene — the forest-road sequence — caught across eight angles: a drone line directly overhead, the drift on a rear fisheye and a road-side rail, CCTV surveillance of the route, and the briefcase timer counting down inside the car. Two more scenes are still in post and stay under wraps for now.
